Features of our application

Simplicity

Customer can access their information quickly,easily and Clear, also it has uncluttered screens with obvious leads to the next step in the app process and no ambiguity which will improve the customer experience and will encourage them to continue to use our application.

Speed

Fast loading screens cause no one likes waiting, particularly when all they have to look at is a screen-loading symbol and this frustration soon gives way to boredom resulting in a decision to seek something better.

Good image resolution

What we have on screen is sufficiently detailed to make the user experience worthwhile.

Flexibility

Our mobile application is available on all operating systems – iOS, Android, and Windows.

User-Friendly, Responsive Interface

Metroid is a user-friendly interface and responsive. Customers have a seamless experience and clearly understand what to do with the app, which buttons to click and so on.

Security

Internet security is becoming an ever-increasing issue so with Metroid security is an absolute must cause we storing personal and sensitive information or credit and debit card details.

Search options

Customer has the ability to search on metro map, stations and prices to make it easier for them while using the application.

User feedback

Feedback is a perfect way to get customers to tell us what they want and enable them to tell us what they think would make it better.

Updates

Always updated so we can respond to security issues as they arise. Updates keep our app fresh and give the user more of what they want.
